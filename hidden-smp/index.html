Description

Hidden SMP is a session-based, modded SMP where players select five mods from any category, except horror, and disguise them so no one knows what they are. Then all mods are combined into a modpack.

The SMP consists of six sessions, each lasting three hours, allowing players to enjoy their adventures at a relaxed pace. While most mods will be chosen by the players, two mods will be automatically added to the modpack, Essential and Simple Voice Chat.

Rules

*Client side mods are mods that the server does not need installed for them to work properly.

**Mods that grant you /op or that can be considered hacking.

  • You may not choose client side mods*
  • You may not choose any horror mods
  • Please try not to choose mods that are known to have alot of incompatibilities
  • No OP** Mods

Server FAQ.

How do i join the server?
To join, you first need to download the Essential mod. Then, you need to friend the user "NeonXL" and wait for the server to start. Once it does, you will get a notification saying that your invited to play. If you miss this notification, go to the multiplayer tab and click friends.
